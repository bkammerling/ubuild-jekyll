Mobility Sessions

We host an informal, invitation-only meet-up for leaders of the European Mobility scene. Our mission is to accelerate the world's transition to sustainable transportation. By providing a platform to startups, corporates, NGOs and research institutions to share and build on each others ideas we hope to get there faster.

Important Events Update

As COVID-19 becomes a matter of global concern, we’re taking some proactive measures to help minimise the chances of spread of the coronavirus. The safety and well-being of our clients, employees, partners and guests has always been a matter of top priority for us - now more than ever before.

In light of growing concerns, we’ve made the difficult decision, as a host, to call off our events scheduled in the next couple of months. This includes the Wunder Tech Sessions on March 26th and the Wunder Mobility Sessions on April 2nd.

We are exploring options to either reschedule the event or conduct an online edition of the same, depending on local realities. We will try to come back to you with more information as soon as possible.

We’re confident that with the right practices, we can help curb the spread of COVID-19. In the meantime, should you have any concerns about the matter, please reach out to us on events@wundermobility.com.

Read more about the WHO's recommendations on best practices here.

Stay safe,
Team Wunder
