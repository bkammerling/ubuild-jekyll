Mobility Sessions

We host an informal, invitation-only meet-up for leaders of the European Mobility scene. Our mission is to accelerate the world's transition to sustainable transportation. By providing a platform to startups, corporates, NGOs and research institutions to share and build on each others ideas we hope to get there faster.
