Carpooling App

Built by the community.

The Wunder carpool app is built on feedback given to us by more than a million users who use the app every single day and is rated 4+ on all app stores!

Match. Interact. Ride.

Create your ride as a passenger or a driver within the same app, and our smart routing algorithms will match you with a list of people travelling in your direction.

Safe, Secure & Reliable.

The app offers driver/passenger's social media profile links, real time ride tracking and government ID verifications in order to maintain end to end security.

Tracking and Measurement

Our Operator Dashboard offers you all metrics such as rides, successful drivers and passengers, service levels, costs etc that would help you stay up to date with all your carpooling operations at the click of a button. All data is available for a variety of time intervals.
