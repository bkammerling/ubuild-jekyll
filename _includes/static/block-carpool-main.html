Technology for carpooling & parking

Carpooling is a fast and fun way for employees to drive to work together. Co-workers on similar routes can share their vehicle, reducing strain on your parking and on the road.

Commuting together

Our app enables employees to find each other and ride together. Fewer vehicles means more parking spaces and less traffic.

Smart parking integration

Give priority parking to employees who carpool, reducing the amount of spaces needed and facility costs.

Promote Corporate Sustainability

Wunder Carpool effectively reduces the environmental impact of commuting.

Employees can view fun facts like their CO2 saved and top carpoolers.

Tracking and Reporting

Real-time insights into your carpool operations and monthly CSR reports which allow you to keep track of your positive impact.
