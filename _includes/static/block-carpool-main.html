From the customisable app and the operations console to launching and optimising operations, our technology and expertise in frontier markets helps us create the most robust carpooling communities.

Carpooling App

Built by the community.

The Wunder carpool app is built on feedback given to us by more than a million users who use the app every single day and is rated 4+ on all app stores!

Match. Interact. Ride.

Create your ride as a passenger or a driver within the same app, and our smart routing algorithms will match you with a list of people travelling in your direction.

Safe, Secure & Reliable.

The app offers driver/passenger's social media profile links, real time ride tracking and government ID verifications in order to maintain end to end security.

Tracking and Measurement

Our Operator Dashboard offers you all metrics such as rides, successful drivers and passengers, service levels, costs etc that would help you stay up to date with all your carpooling operations at the click of a button. All data is available for a variety of time intervals.

Spotlight

The Wunder Carpool OS is already solving problems around the globe.

Pollution

With the growning number of personally owned vehicles, air quality around the world is becoming hazardous.

In New Delhi, one of the most polluted cities in the world, we helped reduce CO2 levels by 20,000 kg last month. And we’re growing.

Congestion

It's not always easy to add extra lanes to roads, but our carpool product introduces another way to decongest.

In Manila, one of the most congested cities in the world, we help keep more than 50,000 cars off the road every single day.

Safety

With city populations breaching 25 million around the world, finding a safe & comfortable community isn't always so easy.

In Rio de Janeiro, one of the most violent cities in the world, we’re creating one of the world’s largest carpooling communities.
