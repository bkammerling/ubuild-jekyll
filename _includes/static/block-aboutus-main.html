Our Journey

14-15

The founding of WunderCar

At the end of 2014, Gunnar and Sam saw an untapped opportunity in the international mobility market. They quickly assembled a motivated team, took the plunge and founded one of the very first ridehailing startups in Germany. WunderCar was born!

16-17

Full speed ahead

After countless market tests, tweaks and late-night strategy sessions, our founders decided to refresh their business model. WunderCar switched the focus to ridesharing in emerging markets, and communities across the globe were introduced to an app taking the (congested) world by storm: Wunder Carpool.

18-19

Expanding horizons

Because We Share. Our mantra, invented in 2018, reflects our core belief that driving the future of shared mobility is a team effort.

By the end of 2019, we had expanded our portfolio from three to seven products in two short years and fulfilled a dream of entering the hardware market with Wunder Vehicles. That’s when it really became official: we were now an all-in-one mobility solution provider!

20

Because we share tomorrow

Today, we’re proud to be building the future of mobility with over 70 clients across six continents.
