Software

The Booking App

Our booking app helps companies allocate available parking spots to employees, guests and applicants as efficiently as possible, bringing space utilization up to almost 100%.

  • Automatic authentification when leaving or entering through license plate recognition software
  • Gate opens after successful authentification
  • Alternative authentification can be completed with a QR scanner
  • Data is sent to the dashboard in real time

Mission Control

Operating Dashboard

In addition to real-time data, analysis functions and user management functions, our dashboard is optimised to provide all parking space information and personal access to our hardware data and controls.

  • Detailed real-time information regarding the status of all spots in the entire parking garage/area is always viewable
  • Available spots and/or incorrectly parked cars can also be managed on the dashboard
  • Data analysis enables prognoses of future parking spot availability
  • If optional digital payment system is selected, all payments and their status can be managed on the dashboard

Hardware Overview

Parking Access Management

Our state-of-the-art automatic parking terminal allows quick and easy access to parking spots through automatic license plate recognition mechanisms and QR code scanners. It's compatible with all existing solutions.

  • Authentification of license plates or QR codes
  • Reliable functionality due to high detection rate
  • Compatible with other existing terminals and can be used at the same time
  • Digital payment systems are available on request
  • Increases flow of traffic, reduces wait times for employees and guests
