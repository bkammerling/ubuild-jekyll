Connectivity

With our built-in IOT technology, you can cut out third-party devices. Our vehicles are engineered to seamlessly communicate with each other — and with you — to support 24/7 connectivity across your mobility ecosystem.

Logistics & Storage

We’ll handle all the sourcing, manufacturing and shipping — we’ll even handle storage in our dedicated facility if you’re low on space. Pick out your vehicle of choice, and we’ll pick it up from there.

Customization

Your vehicles are your vision. Choose your company’s colors and icons and we’ll bring them to life, providing a fully branded experience for your customers and the world.

Wunder Financial Services

Wunder Finance can help you turn your dream fleet into a reality. Flexible financing options and consulting services are available to help you achieve your goals. Pay in installments and get support when you need it so we can make your ride a little smoother.
