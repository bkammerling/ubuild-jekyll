Customer Experience

Plan A Ride

Users can book weeks in advance with a convenient calendar view or on the spot when they see a vehicle.

Choose a Vehicle

Users can select their vehicle type of choice and if they’d prefer to have a full tank or no fuel included.

Confirm the Details

After choosing their vehicle, users can decide on paying in advance or paying at pick-up. Reservation dates are confirmed and then it’s on to the next step.

Convenient Pick Up

The perfect vehicle is never far away. Users choose the nearest pick-up location and can view helpful vehicle details like fuel level.


Once they’ve reached the vehicle, that’s it - users can unlock and drive away!
