Understand your data

Wunder City aggregates data from multiple mobility services into one place. Turn data into customized, visual, and easily-digestible reports to better understand your city’s ecosystem — no prior data knowledge or extra resources required.

Manage your city

View all mobility providers in your city at once and see how their services stack up. Analyze historical data to get an overview of the way the market is developing. Additionally, keep track of demand fluctuations and set parking zones around the city.

City of Hamburg

www.hamburg.de

The City of Hamburg had the goal of improving their infrastructure and paving the way towards a greener future. In order to do that, they needed to get a handle on new mobility modes in the city.

That’s why Hamburg uses reports to create an urban planning framework. The reports visualize data that Hamburg can analyze to make smarter decisions on how to organize the city’s mobility services.
