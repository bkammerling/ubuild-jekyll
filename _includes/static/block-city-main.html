While there is a great deal of promise with new mobility innovations, the emergence of mobility comes with its own set of challenges and considerations for planners, residents and local decision makers. Cities must regulate mobility offerings and take the design of the mobility turnaround into their own hands.

Smart Data for Smart Cities

Wunder City provides a clear overview of mobility activities within the city. It monitors developments, and with insightful visualizations helps make evaluations of the usage of urban mobility offerings.

The tool is created in close collaboration with cities to obtain an overview of the existing offerings from a city-wide perspective, in which the provider reports relevant figures for city stakeholders.
